# Pinterest Launch Checklist

## 1. Create Account
1. Go to https://www.pinterest.com/business/create/
2. Register with your email
3. Set profile name: **DeskVibe**
4. Profile description:
   > Premium desk accessories for the modern workspace. Warm minimalism meets functional beauty. 75+ products, 8 categories. Free shipping over $75.

## 2. Verify Your Site
1. Settings → Claimed Accounts → Claim → Website
2. Choose "Add HTML tag"
3. Copy the meta tag and send it to me (I'll add it to the site code)
