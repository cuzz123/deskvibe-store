# Design

**Register:** brand — this is a premium DTC e-commerce storefront. Design IS the product.

**Brand:** Warm, Material-honest, Curated. Expert confidence + curator's eye + honest premium + restrained warmth. "We chose these materials because they're the right ones," not "this is expensive."

**Palette:** Shop Black (#1c1917) primary, Indigo Ink (#6366f1) accent (≤10% of any screen), white + stone neutral scale. Single font: Figtree at all weights.

**Anti-references:** No marketplace density (Amazon/Walmart grids), no cold SaaS blue-grey, no luxury-fashion pretension. It should feel like walking into a well-lit design studio.

**Principles:** Material speaks first. Show, don't sell. Curate, don't catalog. Warmth through substance.
